PRODUCT VISION
AI-powered search
Having a lot of distributed data & documentation across dozens of tools leads to the fact that it is hard to get a full picture on the subject. Amadeo has a proprietary AI-powered search algorithm that provides you with smart search across all your platforms, so you never miss a thing.
SEE HOW OUR SEARCH WORKS
ESSENCE OF THE SUBJECT
NLP-based summarization
Summarization helps companies overcome information overload, stay on top of the subject and at the same time not lose sight of critical details. Our proprietary NLP algorithm analyzes your data, identifies different subjects, draws connections between them, and summarizes all of the results. Thus, you get a concentrated summary by multiple documents that contains all the key aspects of your subject. This ensures you won’t miss critical details.
