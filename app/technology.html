Proprietary AI-powered search algorithm
The key to an effective search engine is to return results ranked by relevance. Our search engine works on all the data sources (360-degree estimation). This helps on the one hand to quickly find comprehensive and up-to-date information, and on the other hand to get a snapshot of the product state in real-time.
Proprietary NLP-algorithm for creating summaries
Our NLP model creates great summaries by capturing the essence of the content and generating it through an "extractive" technique. The unique aspect of the model is that it works with multidimensional data, i.e. summaries are extracted from multiple documents and data sources. The model scans millions of documents instantly. It is fully automated (automatically reads, organizes, and summarizes the info), supports multi-document & multi-domain summarization, and produces high-quality text that is both coherent and grammatically correct.
