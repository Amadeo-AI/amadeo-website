Amadeo's excellence through innovation

Proprietary AI-powered search algorithm
The key to an effective search engine is to return results ranked by relevance. Our search engine works on all the data sources (360 degree estimation). This helps on the one hand to quickly find comprehensive and up-to-date information, and on the other hand to get a snapshot of the product state in real time.
Proprietary NLP-algorithm for creating summaries
Our NLP model creates great summaries by capturing the essence of a content, and generating it through an "extractive" technique. This enables users to get to the point faster by reading and analyzing only the most important and relevant information. The unique aspect of our NLP model is that it works with multidimensional data, i.e. summaries are extracted from multiple documents and data sources. The model scans millions of documents instantly. It is fully automated (automatically reads, organizes, and summarizes the info) and supports multi-document and multi-domain summarization. It produces high quality text that is both coherent and grammatically correct. At the moment the model generates an extractive summary, but we are currently working on adding an abstractive summary (i.e. text that is paraphrased and adapted by the system).
