Problem we
are solving

Gathering info about the topic of interest, you need to search data manually in each of the data sources that exist in the company. You need to read all of the material, compare between sources and draw conclusions. This takes a lot of time, human resources, attentiveness, familiarity with the product and knowledge of where to search and what to search for. And, even then there is a good chance that important info will be missed or misunderstood; too much data and human error.
